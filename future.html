Strategic Imperatives for Leaders

Three critical actions organizations must take to succeed in the future of work

Invest in Human Capital

66% of employers expect positive ROI within one year from reskilling investments. The constraint isn't capital—it's talent development and retention strategies.

Embrace Technology Strategically

43% of businesses will reduce workforce due to tech integration, while 34% will expand. Strategic technology adoption matters more than organizational size.

Focus on Employee Engagement

Only 42% of employees engage with employer-supported reskilling programs. Better incentives and clear career pathways are critical for success.
