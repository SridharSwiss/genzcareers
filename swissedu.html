Discover Your Future: Top Graduate & PhD Programs in Switzerland

A curated guide to leading Master's and PhD courses across Switzerland's prestigious universities, designed to help you navigate your academic journey.

1. ETH Zurich (Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Zurich)

One of the world's leading universities in science and technology, consistently ranked among the top global institutions. Known for its cutting-edge research and innovation.

Top Master's Programs at ETH Zurich (Examples)

MSc in Robotics, Systems and Control

  • Curriculum Highlights: Focus on autonomous systems, machine learning, control theory, and mechatronics. Interdisciplinary approach with strong research opportunities.
  • Admission Criteria: Excellent Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, or related field. Strong academic record (e.g., minimum 5.0 GPA on a 6.0 scale, or equivalent). English language proficiency (TOEFL iBT 100 / IELTS 7.0). GRE recommended.
  • Estimated Fees: Approx. CHF 730 per semester (for Swiss/EU/EFTA students and potentially others, but check for international student fee changes from Fall 2025). Additional semester fees apply.
  • Next Start Date: Typically Autumn Semester (September). Application deadline usually Dec 1 - Mar 15 for non-EU/EFTA citizens.
  • Application Link: Apply to ETH Zurich Master's
  • Contact Details (General Admissions): master@ethz.ch / +41 44 632 81 00

MSc in Computer Science

  • Curriculum Highlights: Advanced topics in algorithms, artificial intelligence, software engineering, machine learning, and theoretical computer science. Strong emphasis on research and practical application.
  • Admission Criteria: Excellent Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a closely related field. Strong academic performance. English language proficiency (TOEFL iBT 100 / IELTS 7.0).
  • Estimated Fees: Approx. CHF 730 per semester (check for international student fee changes). Additional semester fees.
  • Next Start Date: Typically Autumn Semester (September). Application deadline usually Dec 1 - Mar 15.
  • Application Link: Apply to ETH Zurich Master's
  • Contact Details (General Admissions): master@ethz.ch / +41 44 632 81 00

Top PhD Programs at ETH Zurich (Examples)

PhD in Physics

  • Curriculum Highlights: Doctoral research in theoretical, experimental, or applied physics. Strong integration with research groups and state-of-the-art facilities. Doctoral students are typically employed as scientific assistants.
  • Admission Criteria: Excellent Master's degree in Physics or a related field. Crucially, acceptance by a professor as a supervisor is required before formal application. Strong research potential. English language proficiency.
  • Estimated Fees: Generally low semester fees (e.g., CHF 730 per semester) as PhD students are often employed.
  • Next Start Date: Flexible, usually dependent on the agreement with the supervisor and funding availability.
  • Application Link: Apply to ETH Zurich Doctorate
  • Contact Details (Doctoral Administration): doktorat@ethz.ch

PhD in Computer Science

  • Curriculum Highlights: Independent research in various areas of computer science, including AI, machine learning, systems, networking, and theoretical CS. Close collaboration with faculty and industry partners.
  • Admission Criteria: Excellent Master's degree in Computer Science. Identification of a willing supervisor at ETH Zurich. Demonstrated research aptitude. English proficiency.
  • Estimated Fees: Generally low semester fees.
  • Next Start Date: Flexible, upon agreement with supervisor.
  • Application Link: Apply to ETH Zurich Doctorate
  • Contact Details (Doctoral Administration): doktorat@ethz.ch

2. EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne)

A leading research institution specializing in natural sciences and engineering, known for its strong international outlook and innovation.

Top Master's Programs at EPFL (Examples)

MSc in Data Science

  • Curriculum Highlights: Covers foundations of data science, machine learning, statistics, data management, and big data technologies. Strong links to research labs and industry.
  • Admission Criteria: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related field with excellent grades. English language proficiency (TOEFL iBT 90 / IELTS 6.5 minimum). GRE not generally required but can strengthen application.
  • Estimated Fees: Approx. CHF 730 per semester (for Swiss/EU/EFTA students). Note: For non-resident international students, fees may triple to CHF 2,190 from Fall 2025. Verify current rates.
  • Next Start Date: Typically September (Autumn Semester). Application period usually mid-November to April 30.
  • Application Link: Apply to EPFL Master's
  • Contact Details (Admissions): admission.master@epfl.ch

MSc in Bioengineering

  • Curriculum Highlights: Interdisciplinary program combining engineering principles with biological systems. Specializations in biomedical imaging, neuroengineering, biomaterials, etc.
  • Admission Criteria: Bachelor's degree in a relevant engineering or science discipline. Strong academic record. English language proficiency (TOEFL iBT 90 / IELTS 6.5 minimum).
  • Estimated Fees: Approx. CHF 730 per semester (check for international fee changes from Fall 2025).
  • Next Start Date: Typically September (Autumn Semester). Application period mid-November to April 30.
  • Application Link: Apply to EPFL Master's
  • Contact Details (Admissions): admission.master@epfl.ch

  • Application Link: Apply to EPFL Doctorate
