A Closer Look: Real-World Use Cases

Finance & Insurance

[span_30](start_span)

Financial services are a primary early adopter, with an 86% focus on quantum computing[span_30](end_span).

    [span_31](start_span)[span_32](start_span)
  • **Reinsurance Optimization:** AXA is exploring quantum computing to optimize reinsurance coverage and accelerate risk analysis, aiming for better capital allocation[span_31](end_span)[span_32](end_span).
    • [span_33](start_span)
  • **Fraud Detection:** HSBC and Rigetti are developing quantum machine learning methods for enhanced credit card fraud detection[span_33](end_span).
    • [span_34](start_span)[span_35](start_span)
  • **Secure Transactions:** HSBC and JPMorgan Chase are piloting quantum-safe technologies (QKD/PQC) to protect financial data and transactions[span_34](end_span)[span_35](end_span).

Pharma & Healthcare

[span_36](start_span)

This sector has a balanced focus on computing for R&D (58%) and sensing for diagnostics (42%)[span_36](end_span).

    [span_37](start_span)[span_38](start_span)
  • **Drug Discovery:** Moderna and IBM are using quantum computers to predict mRNA secondary structures, which is critical for designing RNA-based therapeutics[span_37](end_span)[span_38](end_span).
    • [span_39](start_span)[span_40](start_span)
  • **Medical Imaging:** Quantum sensors are being developed for non-invasive, radiation-free cardiac assessments (magnetocardiography) and for early diagnosis of neurodegenerative diseases[span_39](end_span)[span_40](end_span). [span_41](start_span)SandboxAQ is a key player in this area[span_41](end_span).

Automotive & Aerospace

This industry is investing in quantum for complex simulations and next-generation navigation.

    [span_42](start_span)
  • **Aerodynamics Simulation:** Airbus is using quantum computing to solve intricate aerodynamic problems that are too intensive for classical computers, aiming for more fuel-efficient aircraft designs[span_42](end_span).
    • [span_43](start_span)
  • **Traffic Optimization:** Volkswagen has partnered with D-Wave to use quantum annealing for optimizing urban traffic flow, reducing congestion and emissions[span_43](end_span).
    • [span_44](start_span)
  • **GPS-Free Navigation:** The U.S. Air Force and SandboxAQ are testing quantum navigation systems that work in GPS-denied environments by sensing magnetic anomalies in the Earth's crust[span_44](end_span).

The Quantum & AI Flywheel

How AI Enhances Quantum (Today)

[span_45](start_span)

AI is a critical enabling technology that is already accelerating quantum development[span_45](end_span).

    [span_46](start_span)
  • **Smarter Code:** Microsoft's Copilot in Azure Quantum is an AI trained on scientific papers to help researchers write quantum code more efficiently[span_46](end_span).
    • [span_47](start_span)
  • **Error Reduction:** IBM's Qiskit Transpiler Service uses AI to optimize quantum circuits, reducing the number of gates needed for computations, which leads to fewer errors[span_47](end_span).
    • [span_48](start_span)[span_49](start_span)
  • **Cleaner Data:** AI is used to refine and de-noise the data collected from highly sensitive quantum sensors, turning raw measurements into actionable intelligence[span_48](end_span)[span_49](end_span).

How Quantum Will Supercharge AI (Tomorrow)

[span_50](start_span)

Quantum computing promises to overcome the computational limitations of classical systems for AI[span_50](end_span).

    [span_51](start_span)
  • **Faster Training:** Quantum techniques may significantly speed up the training process for deep learning models[span_51](end_span).
    • [span_52](start_span)
  • **New Algorithms:** Quantum mechanics inspires new types of machine learning, such as quantum reservoir computing, which can perform computations infeasible for classical computers[span_52](end_span).
    • [span_53](start_span)[span_54](start_span)
  • **Bridge Technology (LQMs):** Large Quantitative Models (LQMs) running on classical computers can simulate quantum behaviors now, delivering value years before mainstream quantum computers arrive, a strategy emphasized by SandboxAQ's CEO Jack Hidary[span_53](end_span)[span_54](end_span).
