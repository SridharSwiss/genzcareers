Quantum Talent-as-a-Service

[span_34](start_span)

Problem: There's a critical shortage of a quantum-skilled workforce[span_34](end_span). Companies need expertise but can't hire full-time teams.

Solution: An EdTech platform offering certified, industry-specific quantum upskilling modules and a marketplace for freelance quantum consultants.

[span_35](start_span)

Why Now? The report emphasizes building dedicated teams[span_35](end_span), but the talent pool is tiny. A scalable training and staffing solution is a massive, untapped market.

Hybrid Systems Integrator

[span_36](start_span)

Problem: Integrating fragile quantum computers with existing classical IT infrastructure is a major technical challenge[span_36](end_span).

Solution: A middleware software company that builds the "operating system" for the hybrid quantum-classical era, making it seamless for businesses to leverage both.

[span_37](start_span)

Why Now? As more companies run pilot programs[span_37](end_span), the need for practical, easy-to-use integration tools will explode.
